Why the Delivery Stage Matters

The delivery stage is where the consultant's value becomes visible. It's the transformational point in a project where strategy and execution converge to create real impact. In this blog, we break down the 6Cs of consulting delivery:

  • Commence: Setting the stage for success
  • Collect: Gathering insights and information
  • Consider: Analyzing and identifying key findings
  • Create: Designing solutions
  • Counsel: Presenting and guiding implementation
  • Consult: Supporting rollout and ensuring adoption
